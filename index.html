Business Overview

Empowering India's next-gen workforce through innovative, scalable, and inclusive skill development.

AdvaMinds Eduserve Private Limited, based in Pune, connects students with verified coaching centers across academics, competitive exams, vocational skills, and extracurriculars.

Our tech-enabled platform simplifies the search for quality education by offering a range of verified options, personalized recommendations, and digital tools for institutions.

We aim to enhance learning outcomes and make education accessible, affordable, and outcome-driven. With a hybrid learning model, we provide students with both online and offline learning experiences, empowering them to achieve success.

Company Objective

Mission

To connect students with verified coaching centers, enhancing learning outcomes through tech-driven solutions and empowering institutions with tools for effective education.

VISION

To be India's most trusted educational platform, transforming the tutoring ecosystem with accessible, high-quality learning experiences for all.

What if the Right Opportunity Could Change a Life Forever?

Despite India's demographic dividend, millions of youth—especially in rural and semi-urban areas—remain unemployable due to outdated education, lack of soft skills, and limited access to industry-relevant training. The gap between academic learning and workplace readiness continues to hinder economic inclusion and career growth for underserved populations.

What We Provide?

Centralized Platform for Coaching Discovery

Our user-friendly platform enables students to easily find, compare, and enroll in verified coaching centers tailored to their specific needs.

Digital Enablement for Coaching Centers

We provide coaching institutes with essential digital tools, including CRM, student management systems, and digital marketing support to enhance their operations and online visibility.

Personalized Recommendations & Reviews

Leveraging AI-driven recommendations, our platform ensures students are matched with the most suitable coaching options based on their preferences, academic goals, and location, backed by verified reviews & ratings.
